## Design Context

### Users
End-users who upload restaurant menus and interact with an AI to chat about the menu contents. The flow spans two separate pages: a menu upload/extraction interface, and a chat interface. The emotional response should feel modern and tech-forward.

### Brand Personality
Precise, Minimalist, Intelligent.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark mode by default, with a toggle for light mode.
- **Color Palette**: Strictly avoid purple. Use a sleek, tech-focused palette.
- **Style**: Flat minimalist with subtle borders. No heavy shadows, gradients, or 3D effects.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif for general UI elements. Monospace fonts strictly reserved for displaying extracted menu data and technical outputs.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity Through Flat UI**: Rely on subtle borders and spacing rather than heavy shadows or depth to separate elements.
2. **Data Distinction**: Treat extracted menu data as precise information by using monospace fonts, setting it apart from the conversational UI.
3. **Focused Workflow**: Maintain a clear, minimalist transition from the upload phase to the chat phase.
4. **Dark-First Elegance**: Ensure the dark mode looks pristine and tech-forward as the default experience.
